About the Relationships

Love: A deep and affectionate bond between two individuals, symbolizing care and passion.

Friendship: A mutual bond of trust and understanding without romantic involvement.

Marriage: A lifelong partnership where two individuals vow to share their lives.

Siblings: A family bond shared by brothers or sisters, full of love and care.

Affection: A soft and tender feeling of fondness for someone.

Enemies: A state of mutual dislike or rivalry, often stemming from misunderstandings.
